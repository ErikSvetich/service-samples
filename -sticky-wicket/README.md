# Sticky Wicket · Timberline Data samples

Interactive HTML samples for **Sticky Wicket** (Portland). This folder is hosted from the [`service-samples`](https://github.com/ErikSvetich/service-samples) repo (GitHub Pages or other static host).

## Versions

| URL (GitHub Pages) | What it is |
|--------------------|------------|
| [`.../-sticky-wicket/`](https://eriksvetich.github.io/service-samples/-sticky-wicket/) | **V1** · Full interactive “service menu” (Chart.js, KPIs, filters). [Documented as V1](../docs/sample-versions.md#sticky-wicket). |
| [`.../-sticky-wicket/v2/`](https://eriksvetich.github.io/service-samples/-sticky-wicket/v2/) | **V2** · Recommendation-first: headline savings, before/after staffing-style calendar, bullets. Built for operators who already live in **Square** and want *what to do*, not another dashboard. |

## Positioning vs Square

Sales, categories, and tender totals stay in **Square**. These samples show how Timberline layers **schedules, scenarios, and recommendations** on top of exports or APIs. We are not trying to replace Square’s charts; we answer different questions (e.g. “cut this shift, save about $X / month”).

## Source of truth (local)

For day-to-day editing you may keep a working copy under:

`Work\Sticky Wicket\sticky-wicket-data-services-menu.html`

Deploy by copying into this repo’s `-sticky-wicket/index.html` (V1) or maintaining `v2/index.html` separately. See [sample-versions.md](../docs/sample-versions.md) for changelog.
